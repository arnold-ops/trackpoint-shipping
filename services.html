Specialized Cargo Monitoring (IoT Integration)

For your most valuable or sensitive shipments, we provide a layer of IoT (Internet of Things) monitoring that tracks the conditions inside the container, not just its location. Continuous Environment Control: Our advanced sensors monitor and report live data on temperature, humidity, tilt, and shock. This is vital for pharmaceuticals, high-tech electronics, and specialized industrial equipment. Proactive Alerting: If any monitored condition deviates from the predetermined safe range, our system instantly triggers an alert to your Logistics Specialist, allowing for intervention before product integrity is compromised. We secure your investment by proactively mitigating environmental risk.

The Precision Tracking Platform

Our system is built on Multi-Source Data Fusion—a proprietary process that constantly correlates data from every possible source to build a single, verifiable view of your cargo's status and location. Real-Time Data Aggregation: We integrate directly with Global Air Traffic Control (ATC) systems, Vessel AIS Transponders, and Carrier APIs. This bypasses manual scanning errors and gives you the digital truth of your freight’s location at all times. Predictive EDD Engine: Our platform uses machine learning to ingest historical performance data (port dwell times, customs clearance averages, route velocity) and applies it to live conditions (weather, traffic, congestion). This allows us to continuously recalibrate your Estimated Delivery Date (EDD) with a verified accuracy rate of 98.5%, providing the most reliable arrival window possible. Geo-Verification: Every critical milestone, from terminal departure to final delivery, is automatically recorded with geostamped coordinates. This provides an unassailable record of where and when your cargo arrived, eliminating disputes and ensuring absolute accountability.

Services

The core message of Trackpoint Shipping Company is certainty. Every operational note below should reinforce that we do not just move cargo; we manage risk and deliver on our promises, making us the most reliable choice across every segment of the supply chain.

Air Freight

Our Air Freight service is defined by its speed, but its true value is in its schedule reliability. While air is the fastest mode, we eliminate the common pitfalls of cargo "rolling" to a later flight. Trackpoint utilizes exclusive block space agreements (BSA) with premium global air carriers, ensuring booked capacity is always secured capacity. This guarantees your time-critical, high-value, or perishable shipments—from pharmaceuticals to high-tech components—are on the Next Flight Out (NFO), as promised. We provide true 1-to-3 day transit certainty globally, backed by a zero-tolerance policy for manifest errors, which keeps your cargo moving through customs without delays.

Ocean Freight

We transform the unpredictable nature of sea freight into a predictable process. Our reliability in Ocean Freight (FCL and LCL) comes from our sophisticated risk management and deep port relationships. We combat common delays—like port congestion and vessel rescheduling—by leveraging proprietary data analytics to select the most reliable vessel/string routes, often avoiding bottleneck terminals entirely. Every Container ID we handle is monitored with Geofence Technology, giving you an early warning (and an updated EDD) days before a disruption might occur. This proactive approach ensures your bulk, heavy, or non-time-sensitive freight achieves transit times that are not merely cheap, but demonstrably dependable.

Ground Shipping

Ground shipping is the backbone of regional and final-mile logistics. Trackpoint’s reliability on the road is maintained through a dedicated, modern fleet equipped with live GPS and telematics. We do not rely on third-party carrier scans alone; we track the asset itself. This allows for proactive re-routing around traffic and weather, and guarantees on-time pickup and delivery percentages that surpass industry averages. For Less-Than-Truckload (LTL), our terminal network and cross-docking procedures are optimized for efficiency, minimizing handling touches to reduce the risk of damage and ensuring consolidation integrity—meaning your goods move without unnecessary stops or delays.

Warehousing

At Trackpoint, our warehousing services are designed to provide safe, secure and efficient storage solution for your goods. OUr state of the art facilities are strategically located near major transportation hubs, ensuring easy access and convinient logistics management. with flexible storage options, including short term and long term storage, you can choose the solution that best fits your needs. Our expert team is dedicated to providing accurate inventory management, real-time tracking, and monitoring to ensure maximum visibility and control over cargo. Whether you are importing, exporting, or distributing goods within the local market, our ware housing services have got you covered.

Storage

Our Storage solutions are dedicated to maintaining the integrity and security of your inventory before, during, and after transit. Our facilities are strategically located near major transport hubs and feature 24/7 security monitoring, climate-controlled zones (essential for sensitive goods), and fully integrated Warehouse Management Systems (WMS). This technology ensures 99.9% inventory accuracy via automated cycle counting and SKU verification. When your cargo is in our custody, it is under constant, traceable control, acting as a reliable, buffer stock against any unexpected supply chain shifts.

Delivery Van

The final mile is where our reliability promise is finalized. Our branded delivery vans operate on dynamically optimized routing software that plans the most efficient route and provides the customer with a precise two-hour delivery window. This eliminates guesswork. Furthermore, every final delivery is recorded with Electronic Proof of Delivery (e-POD), which includes a recipient signature, a photo of the package at the drop-off location, and a GPS-verified timestamp. Our drivers are highly trained Trackpoint Ambassadors, ensuring the final physical interaction is professional, accurate, and provides you with the indisputable, geostamped accountability needed for immediate closure of your order cycle.
