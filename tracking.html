How to track your package

1

Access the tracking portal

Navigate to the tracking page from the main menu above or the call to action button on the below the hero section. You will be taken directly to Trackpoint's live tracking portal your real time command center for complete shipment visibilty

2

Enter tracking or Reference number

In the search bar, type and or paste either your tracking number(e.g TRK-2024-001) or your reference number providedat the time of booking. both formats are acceptable. Once entered, click the track shipment button to retreive your results instantly

3

View Live Status & Full Timeline

Your shipment's current status appears immidiately - including the official status (e.g "out of delivery", "customs clearance", "Delivered") and your estimated delivery date. Scroll down to explore the complete event timeline - evry check point every location and time stamp from origin to destination, updated in real time.
